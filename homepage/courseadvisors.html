Explore Diverse Computer Science Areas:

Take introductory courses in various areas of computer science (e.g., programming, algorithms, databases) to discover your specific interests. Use this time to fulfill foundational computer science requirements.

Build Programming Skills:

Focus on building strong programming skills, as they are fundamental to success in computer science. Engage with coding projects and practice regularly.

Join Computer Science Clubs:

Join computer science clubs or organizations to connect with peers who share similar interests. Attend events hosted by the computer science department to learn about potential career paths.
